--- layout: post title: 'APEX Listener undocumented feature #1' date: '2010-07-16T21:43:00.001-07:00' author: Kris Rice tags: modified_time: '2012-05-12T10:19:52.233-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-37621059.post-5671683184768276066 blogger_orig_url: http://krisrice.blogspot.com/2010/07/apex-listener-undocumented-feature-1.html --- Seems I always forget to doc everything that I code up.  If you have the apex listener, try this out.

The listener will convert
     http://myhost/apex/builder/home
to
     http://myhost/apex/f?p=builder:home

This hopefully is a baby step to nicer urls to hand out to end users.


http://yourhost.yourdomain/apex/<APP_ALIAS>/<PAGE_ALIAS>
