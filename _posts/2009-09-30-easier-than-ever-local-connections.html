--- layout: post title: Easier than ever local connections date: '2009-09-30T16:51:00.000-07:00' author: Kris Rice tags: modified_time: '2009-10-01T07:52:16.693-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-37621059.post-5155594152702329893 blogger_orig_url: http://krisrice.blogspot.com/2009/09/easier-than-ever-local-connections.html --- I'm constantly trying to make things in the tool easier to get people started working with the database. The first step is always create a connection. You may wonder how can that get easier it's fairly simple as it is.

Well for a local database it is now easier than ever. I added a new context menu "Create Local Connections". When you have a local database it will automatically setup a connection for sys,system and any user which has an open account. So now in roughly 15 seconds you have a connection to each user in your local database. Then just simply open, enter the password and your up an running writing code. I plan on later extending this to something like "create connection based on connection X"
